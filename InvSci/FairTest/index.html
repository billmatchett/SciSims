Fair Test Builder

Build a valid investigation by choosing the independent variable, dependent variable and controlled variables for different scientific questions.

Learning Intention

Students will understand that a fair test changes one independent variable, measures one dependent variable and controls other important variables.

Success Criteria

Students can identify variables, justify controls and decide whether an investigation is valid enough to test a scientific question.

    Teacher notes

    This sim is designed for explicit teaching, retrieval practice or quick formative assessment on experimental design.

    • Common misconception: students often choose the variable they measure as the independent variable.
    • Validity focus: a test is only useful when one planned factor is changed and major confounding variables are controlled.
    • Reliability extension: ask students to add repeats, sample size, averaging and consistent measurement technique to their written method.
    • Challenge: after students complete the presets, ask them to write their own scientific question and swap it with a partner.
